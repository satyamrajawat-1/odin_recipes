Spaghetti Pasta

Description

Spaghetti pasta is a simple and flavorful Italian dish made with boiled pasta and a rich tomato-based sauce. Garlic, onions, herbs, and olive oil enhance the taste, making it a comforting and satisfying meal. It can be topped with cheese or fresh basil for extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Grated cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and oregano; simmer for 5 to 7 minutes.
  4. Drain spaghetti and add it to the sauce.
  5. Mix well and cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with grated cheese on top.
