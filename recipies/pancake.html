Pancakes

Description

Classic pancakes are soft, fluffy, and perfect for breakfast. Made with simple ingredients like flour, milk, eggs, and baking powder, they cook quickly on a hot pan until golden brown. Serve them warm with butter, maple syrup, fruits, or chocolate syrup for a delicious and comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add milk, egg, and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour about 1/4 cup batter onto the pan.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.
